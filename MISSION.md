# MyVinyl Mission Brief

## Vision
MyVinyl is a beautiful, premium, moody vinyl collection manager built for serious collectors and DJs.

## Core Problem We Solve
"I don't remember what my records sound like" — especially when you own 500–2000+ records.

## USP
We don't just catalogue your records. We help you feel them again. MyVinyl combines rich metadata, smart vibe tagging, characterful descriptions, BPM & Camelot key data, and intelligent recommendations so you can always find the right record for your current mood or set.

## Design Direction
- Warm, moody, premium dark aesthetic
- Excellent typography and smooth interactions
- High quality feel throughout
- Beautiful light/dark mode toggle

## Target Users
- Serious vinyl collectors
- Vinyl DJs who play physical records
- People who value mood, vibe and musical memory over just data

## Core Features (Priority Order)
1. Easy ways to get vinyl into the system (Discogs search, manual entry, bulk import)
2. Rich record data (format, condition, BPM, key, genres, vibe tags, descriptions)
3. Beautiful, filterable collection browser
4. Smart "Play Mode" with vibe-based and harmonic recommendations
5. High-quality crate label generator and printing

## Technical Values
- Prioritise beautiful UX over unnecessary complexity
- Keep the core flow fast and delightful
- Always optimise for vinyl-specific needs (formats, condition, physical feel)
